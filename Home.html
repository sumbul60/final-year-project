Welcome To Dining Hall Management System

The Dining Hall Management System will be used to deal with the problems on managing hostel mess activities and avoid the problems which occur when carried manually.It is used to improve clerical services for all the students and workers of the hostel mess department. The system will help the admin to manage the attendance of the students, inventory management, and mess menu and student fees and dues record. This will also help student to check mess menu and hostel fees and dues.
